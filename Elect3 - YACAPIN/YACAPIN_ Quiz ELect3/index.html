About

Clara Benin is touted as the indie darling of the Philippines. She is one of the first artists under Sony Music's new
regional label OFFMUTE.

Her signature angelic voice and melodic acoustic guitar playing bring a kind of serenity that her listeners
gravitate towards.

Widely loved for her warm and quiet demeanor, the acoustic-folk artist has played in notable festivals like
Wandedand Arts and Music Festival in the Philippines, Music Matters in Singapore, and Zandari Festa in South
Korea.

Clara finds the courage to embrace self-acceptance in the face of adversity on her sophomore studio album,
befriending my tears.

The introspective record serves as the follow-up release to 2020's Fragments, a 5-track EP that maps out different
seasons of her life during the pre-pandemic days.
